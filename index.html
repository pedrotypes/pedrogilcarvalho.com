Hi, I'm Pedro.

I'm a dad, a husband, and I've been a professional programmer for over 20 years.

Now my focus is on helping others work at their best, and develop the organizations where they thrive.

I believe in a few keys to that. Assembling cross-functional teams with the right skills, led by high-ownership individuals. Encouraging them to understand their domain and technologies deeply. Giving them space to focus and deliver. Providing feedback and guidance. Shaping the organization so that there are clear growth paths, and as little day-to-day friction as possible.

I also believe that erring on the side of good engineering practices is better than cutting corners. In my experience, software development teams that value quality outperform others consistently.
